Championing Environmental Justice in Nepal

The Environment Law Society (ELS) Nepal is a premier non-profit organization dedicated to environmental protection. Our society brings together a dedicated group of seven legal professionals — practicing advocates and consultant lawyers who collectively unite under the ELS Nepal platform with the shared objective of promoting, advocating for, and strengthening the implementation of environmental law in Nepal. we actively welcome collaboration with legal professionals, conservationists, researchers, and international partners who share our commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development.

At the mean time, each member of ELS Nepal maintains an independent legal practice. Clients may engage individual members for professional legal representation and consultation on personal legal matters, where the advocates provide diligent and principled assistance grounded in legal expertise and ethical practice.
