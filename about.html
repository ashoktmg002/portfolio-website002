Founded in the heart of Kathmandu, the Environment Law Society (ELS) Nepal is a premier non-profit organization dedicated to environmental protection. Officially registered with the Government of Nepal on 2077/08/17 BS, we have spent over a decade building a foundation of legal advocacy for the earth.

We operate with full transparency and are officially recognized by the Kathmandu Metropolitan City and the tax authorities with a Permanent Account Number (PAN) registration, ensuring our commitment to professional and fiscal accountability.

Our society brings together a dedicated group of seven legal professionals — practicing advocates and consultant lawyers who collectively unite under the ELS Nepal platform with the shared objective of promoting, advocating for, and strengthening the implementation of environmental law in Nepal.

Beyond our local impact, we actively welcome collaboration with legal professionals, conservationists, researchers, and international partners who share our commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development.

Our Purpose

Legal Advocacy for a Greener Future

Our organization is built on the belief that a sustainable future requires a robust legal framework. As outlined in our Constitution (Bidhan), our core objectives drive every initiative we undertake.

01

Driving Sustainable Development

Implementing legal strategies that balance progress with environmental preservation, ensuring future generations inherit a thriving Nepal.

02

Global & National Collaboration

Actively coordinating with national government bodies, international organizations, and diplomatic missions to address climate and environmental challenges.

03

Legal Empowerment

Utilizing the law as a primary tool to safeguard natural resources and ensure environmental justice for all citizens of Nepal.
